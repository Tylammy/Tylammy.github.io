All About Me!

I was born and rasied in Georgia. A majority of my life revolves around the arts.
From a young age, I picked up piano while drawing dogs, cats, and starfishes
on the side. Eventually, in middle school, I started taking art seriously. This was
also around the period where I started learning the violin as well! Only having less
than a year of experience in the violin, I decided to audition for art and orchestra at a
performing arts school near me. Fortunetly, I was accepted into both programs!
Currently, I attend that performing arts high school where I major in playing the violin
and minor in art. In my free time, I really enjoy piano, playing ukulele, animating,
composing music, listening to music, cooking, reading, and journaling/scrapbooking!
